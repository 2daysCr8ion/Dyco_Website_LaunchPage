Waste Streams We Treat

From burden to by-product

Across the most challenging hydrometallurgical and mining waste streams — converting environmental liabilities into recoverable value.

Sulfate Sulfate Waste Brines

High-sulfate waste streams from hydrometallurgical leach circuits converted back into usable sulfuric acid and base.

→ H₂SO₄ & base recovered

Chloride Chloride Waste Brines

High-salinity chloride effluents from mining and processing operations split back into HCl and caustic.

→ HCl & NaOH recovered

Acid Acid Mine Drainage

Acidic drainage from active and legacy mine sites treated to recover acid and neutralise the remaining stream.

→ Acid recovered / Neutralised water

Sulfate Tailings Pore Water

Sulfate-laden interstitial water from tailings storage facilities processed to recover acid and clean water.

→ Acid recovered / Clean water

Chloride Metal Plating Wastewater

Chloride and sulfate-rich rinse waters and spent bath solutions from electroplating and metal finishing operations converted back into process-ready acid and base.

→ HCl & caustic recovered

Mixed Mixed Industrial Brines

Complex multi-source waste brines from industrial operations where conventional neutralisation and disposal is costly and generates ongoing liability.

→ Acid & base recovered
